Intro

From the moment I wrote my first line of code, I knew I’d found something that truly resonated with me. Programming started as a hobby, but it quickly became a passion that has shaped my academic and professional path. With a Bachelor's in Computer Science under my belt and currently working towards my Master’s, I’m committed to continually expanding my expertise.

As a software engineer, I’m driven by the idea that technology should solve problems and make life easier. I enjoy the challenge of taking complex issues and finding elegant, efficient solutions—whether I’m building large-scale applications or experimenting with new ideas on the side.

I’m especially interested in the intersection of machine learning and user experience. There’s so much potential to innovate in these areas, and I’m excited about the possibilities to create tools and platforms that have a real impact.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me focused on fitness and nutrition, two areas that keep me grounded and energized.
